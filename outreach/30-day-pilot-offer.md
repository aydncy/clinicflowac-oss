# ClinicFlowAC — 30-Day Structured Workflow Pilot

## Overview

ClinicFlowAC is offering a 30-day no-cost pilot to selected small clinics.

This pilot focuses on operational clarity and workflow execution — not system replacement.

---

## What This Is

- A structured workflow layer for appointments
- Document checklist enforcement
- Structured consent recording
- Append-only event logging
- Exportable proof packs for audit readiness

---

## What This Is NOT

- Not an EHR replacement
- Not a system migration
- Not a contract commitment
- Not a billing system

---

## Pilot Scope (30 Days)

- Up to 100 appointments
- Structured workflow tracking
- Document checklist enforcement
- Consent event recording
- Proof pack export per appointment

---

## What We Measure

- Missed appointment rate
- Document completion rate
- Consent coverage rate
- Workflow completion time
- Audit exportability

---

## What We Ask From You

- Minimal operational participation
- Anonymous metric sharing
- Short feedback call (15–20 minutes)
- Optional reference quote if satisfied

---

## Data & Privacy

- No sensitive health data required
- No data shared publicly
- Simulation-based demo available if preferred

---

## Cost

Free for 30 days.
No obligation.
